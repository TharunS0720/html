Ashok Leyland

Ashok Leyland is an Indian multinational automotive manufacturer headquartered in Chennai, India. It is one of the largest commercial vehicle manufacturers in the country.

Key Features

  • Headquartered in Chennai, India
  • Part of the Hinduja Group
  • Major producer of trucks and buses
  • Strong presence in defense and commercial markets
  • Focus on innovation and sustainable transport
