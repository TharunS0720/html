Innovations & Technologies

Ashok Leyland is at the forefront of innovation in the commercial vehicle industry. From advanced engine technologies to cutting-edge telematics, the company is committed to delivering vehicles that offer superior performance, efficiency, and safety.

iALERT - Fleet Management Solution

iALERT is Ashok Leyland's advanced fleet management system that provides real-time data on vehicle performance, driver behavior, and maintenance alerts, helping fleet operators optimize their operations.

Electric Mobility

With a focus on sustainability, Ashok Leyland is expanding its electric vehicle portfolio, offering zero-emission solutions for urban and intercity transportation. The company is set to launch a new plant in Uttar Pradesh in 2025, aiming to produce 2,500 electric buses annually, with plans to double capacity over the next decade.

Autonomous Vehicles

In collaboration with Minus Zero, Ashok Leyland is developing self-driving trucks tailored for ports, factories, and corporate campuses. This partnership aims to revolutionize logistics by integrating autonomous technology into commercial vehicles.
